Our mission, our team, and the milestones that brought us here.

Our story

GreenTech Solutions started in 2019 in a borrowed Albuquerque garage with one prototype: an energy monitor that could pay for itself in fourteen months.

We were tired of climate conversations that ended at intentions. The businesses we wanted to help — the dental practices, the regional distributors, the family-owned manufacturing shops — weren't asking for moral clarity. They were asking for a tool, a number, and a next step.

Six years later, more than a thousand small and mid-sized businesses use our hardware and software to measure, cut, and report their environmental impact. The pitch hasn't changed: sustainability has to be operational, not aspirational.

Milestones

The moments that shaped where GreenTech is today.

  1. 2019

    Founded in Albuquerque

    First prototype of the EcoSense energy monitor, built nights and weekends in a borrowed garage.

  2. 2020

    First product ships

    Twelve pilot customers across New Mexico small businesses go live with EcoSense in production.

  3. 2022

    Smart recycling launches

    IoT-connected sort bins released to market. Customer base passes one hundred businesses.

  4. 2023

    Series A funding

    Closed an eight-million-dollar round led by Catalyst Climate Ventures. Opened a second office in Denver.

  5. 2024

    Carbon tracking SaaS

    Launched the platform that turns operational data into audit-ready emissions reporting.

  6. 2025

    One thousand customers

    Crossed one thousand businesses served. Certified as a B Corporation in the same quarter.
